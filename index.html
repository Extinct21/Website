What is Chess?

Chess is a two-player board game that is played on a square board consisting of 64 squares arranged in an 8x8 grid. The game is played with 16 pieces for each player, including one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns. The game involves strategic thinking, planning, and tactical maneuvering as players aim to control the board and outmaneuver their opponent.
